&128170; Why your body needs exercises/h2>

Exercise is important for keeping your body strong and healthy. It helps you build muscles, improve your heart health, and boost your mood. Regular physical activity can also help you maintain a healthy weight and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

Dancing, swimming, and running are great ways to stay active and healthy.

☀ Quick health tips

  • 🍎 Eat 5 fruits and veggies every day
  • 💧 Drink 8 glasses of water daily
  • 😴 Sleep 9 to 11 hours every night
  • 🏃 Move your body for 60 minutes
